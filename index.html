About The Conference

What Is Imperium MUN?

Imperium MUN is a student-led conference focused on collaboration, critical thinking, and impactful debate. Delegates take on real-world global issues and develop the skills needed to lead with confidence.

Location: Riyadh, Saudi Arabia

Questions

Frequently Asked 💬

Click any card to flip it and reveal the answer.

🧠

Do I need previous MUN experience?

No experience needed. Beginners are fully welcome — guidance materials will be shared before conference days.

🙌

Who can join Imperium MUN?

Students in Riyadh interested in diplomacy, debate, leadership, and global affairs. All backgrounds welcome.

📅

When will applications open?

As soon as committee assignments are finalized. Join the early access list to be the first to know.

⚡

How fast do you reply to questions?

Usually within 24 hours on email and Instagram DMs. For urgent matters, DM us directly on Instagram.

💰

Is there a participation fee?

Fee details will be announced when applications open. Sign up for early access to stay in the loop first.

🏫

Do I need school permission to join?

We recommend letting your school know. We're happy to provide an official letter if your institution requires one.

🌍

What topics will committees cover?

Committees tackle real-world issues — from climate and security to economic policy. Specific topics announced soon.

🎓

Will I receive a certificate?

Yes. All participating delegates receive an official Imperium MUN certificate of participation.
