About The Summit

What Is The Enchanted Summit?

Enchanted is a debate summit rooted in fictional and otherworldly topics to bring out the passion of participants in the debate community. It focuses on unity through skills rather than competition.

Students are given mentorship, support, and guidance to grow skills such as:

  • 🎤 Public Speaking
  • 🗣️ Debate
  • 🔍 Research
  • 📋 Organization
