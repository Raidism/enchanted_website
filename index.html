Students in Riyadh interested in diplomacy, debate, leadership, and global affairs.

📅 When will applications open?

As soon as committee assignments and process details are finalized. Join the early list for first notice.

⚡ How fast do you reply to questions?

Usually within 24 hours on email and Instagram direct messages.
