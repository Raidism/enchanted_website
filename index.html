About The Summit

What Is The Enchanted Summit?

A debate summit rooted in fictional and otherworldly topics to bring out the passion of participants in the debate community. It is a way to unite about the skills, not the competition. Students will be given mentorship, help, and support to empower growth of skills such as public speaking, debate, organization, research, and more!
